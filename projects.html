LiDAR Point Cloud Data Project

During my internship with COM-IoT, I used supervisely to label point cloud data frames from both a stationary LiDAR sensor that captured data from a busy street and a mobile LiDAR attached to the roof of a traffic vehicle. Supervisely is a machine learning platform that aids in the development of artificial intelligence solutions. Through supervisely, I was able to annotate over 1,200 PCD frames -- including pedestrians, trucks, buses, cars, motorcycles, traffic cones, bikes, and more. This was an incredible experience as it demonstrated to me how algorithms can learn from a knowledge base so that they may develop their own understanding of real life objects. Utilizing this ability, we can increase traffic safety and enforce safe driving on public streets. To view the workspace holding my annotated PCD frames, click here.
